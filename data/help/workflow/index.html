Level-Specific Workflows

The workflows are different based on the level of abstraction. Higher levels of abstraction (like ASMB5) build on lower levels of abstraction (like Microcode). More programming tools exist at higher levels of abstraction (e.g., batch input/output) than at lower levels of abstraction. The workflow is impacted by the availability of these additional programming tools.
